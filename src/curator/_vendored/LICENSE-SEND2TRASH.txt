send2trash — Send files to OS trash / Recycle Bin
Original source: https://github.com/arsenetar/send2trash

Curator vendors a Windows-only subset of send2trash 1.8.3:
    * curator/_vendored/send2trash/__init__.py        (top-level dispatch)
    * curator/_vendored/send2trash/exceptions.py      (TrashPermissionError)
    * curator/_vendored/send2trash/util.py            (preprocess_paths)
    * curator/_vendored/send2trash/win/__init__.py    (Windows entry point)
    * curator/_vendored/send2trash/win/legacy.py      (ctypes-based, no pywin32)

The macOS implementation, the Linux GIO/freedesktop implementations, and
the pywin32-based ``modern.py`` Windows path are NOT included in this
Phase Alpha vendor. Phase Beta will expand to other platforms as needed.

The unmodified upstream archive lives at
``Github/03_send2trash/send2trash-master/`` for reference.
